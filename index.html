About Me

I am an Oregon based developer that recently completed the University of Oregon full stack coding bootcamp. After graduating from college at San Francisco State University, I worked as an Operations Manager for a financial startup in the San Francisco Bay Area. While working at SmartPay, I worked hand and hand with the product developers to create/troubleshoot web applications. It was during this time I realized that I wanted to transition careers to a full stack developer. With my experience as an Operations Manger and new found knowledge of full stack coding gives me a well-rounded perspective of all aspects of a project.
